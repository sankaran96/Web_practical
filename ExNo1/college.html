About Us

Government Arts College (GAC) in Chidambaram, established 1982, is a public college affiliated with Thiruvalluvar University offering UG, PG, M.Phil, and Ph.D. programs in Arts, Commerce, and Science, known for its dedicated faculty, decent infrastructure, and focus on holistic student development through clubs, NSS, and placement support, located at C-Mutlur, Chidambaram.

Key Information:

  • Location: C-Mutlur, Chidambaram, Cuddalore District, Tamil Nadu.
  • Established: 1982.
  • Affiliation: Annamalai University.
  • Accreditation: NAAC 'B' Grade..
  • Type: Public

Admissions

Admissions are primarily merit-based, determined by performance in qualifying examinations (Class 12 for UG, Graduation for PG).
